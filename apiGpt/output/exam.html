שאלות פתוחות

Describe the role of the hyperplane in Support Vector Machines.

The hyperplane in SVM acts as a decision boundary that separates different classes in the feature space.

Explain the concept of overfitting in machine learning.

Overfitting occurs when a model learns the training data too well, capturing noise along with the underlying pattern, resulting in poor generalization to new data.

What are the advantages of using ensemble methods in machine learning?

Ensemble methods improve the accuracy of models by combining predictions from multiple models, thus reducing the risk of overfitting and increasing robustness.
