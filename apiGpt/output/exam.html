שאלות פתוחות

Explain the relationship between sin(θ) and cos(θ) in terms of their complementary angles.

sin(θ) = cos(90° - θ) and cos(θ) = sin(90° - θ); they are co-functions.

How does the identity tan²(θ) + 1 = sec²(θ) relate to the Pythagorean theorem?

This identity represents the relationship between the sides of a right triangle in the context of trigonometric functions, analogous to a² + b² = c².

Describe the impact of integration by parts on solving integral expressions.

Integration by parts is a technique that transforms a complex integral into simpler parts, making it easier to evaluate, based on the product rule for differentiation.
